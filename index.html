The woman, the artist, the legend

The Mexican artist Frida Kahlo is famously known for her self-portraits and her life which was filled with passion, pain, and bold vibrant colours.

She is celebrated in her native homeland of Mexico and across the world for her attention to Mexican and indigenous culture and for her depiction of the female experience and form.

Her art was often colourful and painful and she continues to inspire many long after her passing.
